#### From [Byron Knoll](http://byronknoll.blogspot.com/2011/03/vector-playing-cards.html)

[The source SVGs](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Playing_cards_set_by_Byron_Knoll) are also on Wikimedia Commons.
